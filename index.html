PDEU – ISRO · CubeSat Mission

CubeSat ADCS — Attitude Determination & Control

Working on the ADCS subsystem of a LEO CubeSat mission under PDEU–ISRO collaboration, implementing attitude estimation and control algorithms — TRIAD, Extended Kalman Filter (EKF), B-Dot, and PID — with sensor fusion across magnetometers, gyroscopes, and sun sensors, integrated with magnetorquers and reaction wheels. The payload is designed for night-time light imaging, enabling urban growth monitoring and disaster response analytics from orbit.
